Make a plant with just a fruit


All you need is just a glass of water, a rope and an avocado. Follow these steps:

  1. Clean the avocado pit thoroughly
  2. Insert 3-4 toothpicks around the middle of the pit
  3. Suspend the pit in a glass of water
  4. Place in a sunny spot and wait for roots to grow
  5. Once roots appear, plant in soil

Remove the avocado pit and clean it thoroughly

Step 2: Prepare for water

Insert toothpicks and suspend in water

Step 3: Wait for growth
